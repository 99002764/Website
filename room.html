ROOMS IN RESORT

Rooms kept under out of order are not sellable and these rooms are deducted from the hotels inventory.

Maintenance codes are used in PMS / hotel management system to block a room under maintenance. This helps to easily identify why the room was put under maintenance.

A room may be out-of-order for a variety of reasons, including the need of maintenance, refurbishing and extensive cleaning etc.

Rooms kept under out of service are not deducted from the hotel inventory.

This is a temporary blocking and reasons may be bulb fuse, T V remote not working, Kettle not working etc
